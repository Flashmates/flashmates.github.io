ABOUT

My name is Amrit Shenava and Flashmates is a experimental project that helps folks to find a roommate and a place to live off-campus. Currently in Beta and testing this out in Kent, Ohio.

THE PROBLEM

Moving to a new country, new city or a new place is a very challenging process. A very important task is to hunt for a house and finding a roommate. This is a very lengthy process and a lot of consideration is put in the process. From budget, location to roomate preference, this can be a challenging task.

SO WHAT IS THE SOLUTION?

Flashmates is a project that wants to make finding a off-campus housing and/or a roommate a more efficient process that requires less time.

BETA

Flashmates is currently under Beta Stage and access is only through invite. For those in Kent, Ohio who are looking for a place to stay and/or a roommate, please drop me a mail.
